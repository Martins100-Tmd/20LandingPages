Direction

1. Cook the Jasmine Rice and Saut√© the Ingredients

Start by cooking the jasmine rice. Rinse it under cold water until the water runs clear, then add it to a pot with 1.5 cups of water. Bring it to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and let it simmer for 15 minutes until tender. While the rice is cooking, heat a skillet or wok over medium-high heat with a drizzle of oil, and cook diced chicken breast until golden and fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the pan, then add minced garlic and sliced bell peppers, stirring until fragrant and slightly softened. Toss in fresh spinach and cook until wilted before returning the chicken to the pan.

radio_button_unchecked

2. Add the Teriyaki Sauce and Let It Glaze the Ingredients

Next, pour teriyaki sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. Let it cook for a couple of minutes until the sauce thickens and glazes the ingredients. If you want extra flavor, add a splash of soy sauce or sprinkle in chili flakes for some heat. The rich aroma and vibrant colors will make your dish irresistible!

radio_button_unchecked

3. Plate the Dish and Add Final Garnishes Before Serving

Finally, plate the dish by serving the stir-fry over the freshly cooked jasmine rice. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired, and enjoy this simple, delicious meal that's packed with bold flavors and fresh ingredients!
